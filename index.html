About Me
- Who am I -

Right from my childhood, I have enjoyed observing my surroundings quite closely. The overhead electrical lines, remote control cars, toys, and various other electrical equipment caught my eye at a very young age. Frequent visits to my cousin's workplace, where he repairs mobile phones and other electronic devices, further enhanced this penchant for electronics and electricals.
These experiences created an urge towards electronics, leading me to start my journey as a hobbyist from a young age. I designed timer circuits and programmed Arduinos, sitting corner of my room inhaling the Soldering smoke at midnights. Now, I am a professional hardware design engineer working on designing complex processor-based designs.
  I believe that my enthusiasm for electronics helped me build the required knowledge by surfing YouTube videos, taking Udemy courses, and participating in electronics forums. Consider me if you desire someone with a "can do " and "let's do it " attitude and who enjoys challenges.
